Irrigation

Sprinkler & Irrigation Services

We work on all major residential irrigation systems — diagnosing problems before replacing parts, repairing what's broken, and installing new systems when needed. If it's underground and it waters things, we handle it.

Sprinkler repairHead replacement Valve replacementTimer programming Leak detectionSystem diagnostics Seasonal startupWinterization Drip irrigationFull installation

A lot of irrigation problems look more complicated than they are — and some are worse than they look. We always start with a proper diagnostic so you know exactly what you're dealing with before any work begins.

All quotes are free and come with no obligation. We'll walk you through what we found and what it takes to fix it.

💧 Repair & Diagnostics

We identify what's actually wrong — broken heads, stuck valves, wiring issues, controller problems — and fix the root cause, not just the symptom.

🔧 Valve & Controller Work

Valve replacement, timer reprogramming, controller upgrades, and zone troubleshooting for systems that aren't cycling right.

🔍 Leak Detection

Underground leaks, wet spots, and pressure drops diagnosed properly. Minor repairs to major line breaks — we handle all of it.

🌱 Full Installation

New irrigation systems installed for small yards up to large custom properties. Designed for coverage efficiency and long-term reliability.

🗓 Seasonal Services

Spring startup, system checks, and fall winterization to protect your investment and keep things running through Oregon's weather.

💦 Drip Irrigation

Targeted drip systems for garden beds, planter areas, and water-sensitive zones where traditional sprinklers don't make sense.

Exterior Cleaning

Pressure & Soft Washing

Oregon's wet climate is rough on exterior surfaces. Moss, algae, black streaks, and years of grime build up fast. We use the right combination of pressure and cleaning chemistry for each surface type — effective cleaning without damage.

Driveway cleaningPatio & deck Siding soft washFence & gate Concrete surfacesRV washing Mobile homeFull property refresh

High pressure on the wrong surface causes real damage — stripped paint, cracked wood, forced water behind siding. We match pressure and detergent to the material, so concrete gets treated like concrete and cedar gets treated like cedar.

Most jobs can be estimated with a few photos. Send us what you're working with and we'll put together a quote.

🚗 Driveways & Concrete

High-pressure surface cleaning for concrete driveways, aprons, sidewalks, and parking pads. Removes oil stains, moss, algae, and embedded grime.

🏡 Siding Soft Wash

Low-pressure soft washing with appropriate detergents for vinyl, wood, and painted surfaces. Removes algae, mold, and oxidation without causing damage.

🪵 Patios & Decks

Surface cleaning calibrated for wood, composite, pavers, and concrete patio surfaces. Restores appearance and removes organic growth.

🚌 RV & Mobile Homes

Full exterior soft washing for RVs, mobile homes, and manufactured housing. Safe for painted surfaces and aluminum panels.

🚪 Fences & Gates

Wood and vinyl fence cleaning to remove mold, algae, and weathering. Makes old fencing look considerably better before any staining or sealing.

🏘 Full Property Refresh

Complete exterior cleaning packages covering driveway, siding, patio, and more in a single visit. Good for prepping a home for sale or a full seasonal reset.
